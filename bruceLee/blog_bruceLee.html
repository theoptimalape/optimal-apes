The Mind Body Connection

  • A. Josef Rodriguez | 11/15/2021

Fitness influencers and celebrity health advocates are found by the shit ton today. Health and wellness have become a topic of great interest. Not only is there a physical benefit to aesthetics and mobility, but it also provides and economic opportunity. Today we dive deeper into the territory of the abstract, and deeply impactful benefits that also come along with physical training.

One of the greatest minds to ever explore this crossroads between fitness and philosophy was none other that Bruce Lee. He was well aware of a mind, body connection occuring in us all. Much like a muscle, it is something that can be trained and refined, bringing forth a range of physiological benefits.

"By adopting a certain physical posture, a resonant chord is struck in spirit."
- Bruce Lee

The quote above from Lee depicts that connection. Conditioning the body to become strong, resilient, and capable, will simultaneously condition the spirit to do the same. It is not the knowing, or hearing of wisdom that makes us grow, but the doing, and living by its principles. Sometimes it is unappealing to get out of bed, and get the day going. We may fee like we need some ispiration, but as in this example, it is the getting out of bed and getting going that are inspiring.

Taking on our responsibilities and challenges head on, allthough intimidating, are the most rewarding decisions we can make. Our situations dramatically improve as a result of effort, and that alone can be inspirational. But no matter the amount of information or knowledge we have, action must take place before reward.
